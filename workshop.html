What is the WHATWG?
The Web Hypertext Application Technology Working Group (WHATWG) is a growing comunity of people interested in evolving the Web. It focuses primarily on the development of HTML and APIs needed for Web applications.
The WHATWG's main focus is HTML5. The WHATWG also work on Web workers and occasionally specifications outside WHATWG space are discussed on the WHATWG mailing list and forwarded when appropriate.
How can I get involved?
There are lots of ways you can get involved, take a look and see what you xan do!
Is participation free?
Yes, everyone can contribute. There are no memberships fees involved, it's an open process. You may easily subscribe to the WHATWG mailing lists. You may also join the W3C's new HTMLWG by going though the slighly longer application process.
