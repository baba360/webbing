"The Twelve Ceasers" by Suetonius

Suetonius (or Gaius Suetonius Tranquilius) was born circa A.D. 70 and died sometime after A.D. 130. He composed a history of the twelve Caesers from Julius to Domitian (died A.D. 96). His work was a significant contribution to the best-selling novel and television series "I, Claudius." Suetonius' work includes biographies of the following Roman emperors:

  • Julius
  • Augustus
  • Tiberius
  • Gaius
  • Claudius
  • Nero
  • Galba
  • Otho
  • Vitellius
  • Vespasian
  • Titus
  • Domitian
